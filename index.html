I’m Iker Fernández, a UX and UI designer from Spain currently living in Barcelona and working at Sage.

After graduating with two higher-education diplomas – Computer Applications Development and Graphic Advertising – I have honed my professional experience for nearly a decade in the visual design area, working as a freelance designer and collaborating with several agencies in Italy, Spain, and the US.

These past few years have provided me with the opportunity to broaden my visual communications knowledge while working on UX and UI projects, branding, editorial design, and Product Design. Besides that, I have been involved in launching and collaborating with a wide list of startups and entrepreneurs, shaping their ideas through design.

I have worked with some remarkable companies– Best Self, Typeform, Fiat, Upward Magazine, Yoigo, Sanitas, and Multiópticas - to name a few. I truly enjoy every project and I love facing new challenges, learning from them and from the people that surround me.
